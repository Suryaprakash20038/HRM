## For HR Users

### Generating a Letter (3 Simple Steps)

#### Step 1: Open Letter Generator
1. Go to **Recruitment** ‚Üí **Candidates**
2. Find the candidate you want to send a letter to
3. Click the **eye icon** to view candidate details
4. Click **"Generate Call Letter"** button

#### Step 2: Select a Design
You'll see 5 professional templates:

- Click on any template card to select it
- The selected template will be highlighted
- You'll see a "Design Locked" message
- Click **"Next: Enter Content"**

#### Step 3: Fill in the Details
Enter the following information:

**Required Fields:**
- **Candidate Name**: Auto-filled from candidate data
- **Designation**: Job role (e.g., "Senior Software Engineer")
- **HR Manager Name**: Your name
- **Body Content**: Main letter text (plain text only)

**Optional Fields:**
- **Joining Date**: For offer/appointment letters
- **Salary**: Annual CTC

**Body Content Tips:**
- Write in plain text (no formatting)
- Press Enter twice to create new paragraphs
- HTML tags will be automatically removed
- Keep it professional and clear

**Example Body Content:**

#### Step 4: Generate & Send
- Review your content
- Click **"Generate & Send PDF"**
- The system will:
  - Create a professional PDF with your selected design
  - Email it to the candidate
  - Update the candidate status

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I use bold or italic text?
**A:** No, the system only accepts plain text. This ensures consistency across all letters.

### Q: What if I make a mistake?
**A:** Click the "Back to Design" button to return to Step 1, or click "Cancel" to close the modal.

### Q: Can I preview before sending?
**A:** Currently, the PDF is generated and sent immediately. A preview feature may be added in future updates.

### Q: How do I create paragraphs?
**A:** Press Enter twice (leave a blank line) between paragraphs.

### Q: Can I save a draft?
**A:** Not currently. Complete the letter in one session.

### Q: What happens after I click "Generate & Send"?
**A:** The system:
1. Creates a PDF using your selected design
2. Merges your content into the template
3. Emails the PDF to the candidate
4. Updates the candidate status to "Selected"

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Choose the Right Design**
   - Classic: Government, legal, formal sectors
   - Modern: Tech, IT, digital companies
   - Professional: Banking, finance, consulting
   - Creative: Marketing, design, media
   - Minimal: Startups, modern brands

2. **Write Clear Content**
   - Keep sentences short and clear
   - Use professional language
   - Double-check spelling and grammar
   - Include all necessary details

3. **Use Proper Formatting**
   - Start with a greeting
   - Use paragraphs for different topics
   - End with a call to action
   - Include contact information if needed

4. **Verify Information**
   - Double-check candidate name
   - Verify salary figures
   - Confirm joining dates
   - Review all details before sending

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Please select a design template"
**Solution:** You must select one of the 5 templates before proceeding to Step 2.

### Issue: "Please fill all required fields"
**Solution:** Check that you've filled in:
- Candidate Name
- Designation
- HR Manager Name
- Body Content

### Issue: HTML tags appearing in my text
**Solution:** Don't worry! The system automatically removes HTML tags. Just type plain text.

### Issue: Letter not sent
**Solution:** 
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify the candidate has a valid email address
3. Try again or contact IT support
